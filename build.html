Mohnani Building is a 10-story luxury mixed-use development located near the historic Port of Yokohama and Yamashita waterfront park. We are located within a 3-minute walk to 5-star hotels, restaurants, luxury retailers, major banks, post-office, government offices, parks, and the subway.


Our apartments have been specifically designed to meet the housing needs of the foreign executive. Traditionally our residents have been executives of multi-national American, European, and Japanese firms. These apartments are available for rent furnished or unfurnished on either a short or long term lease.

Features:

  • Apartment sized to international standards - 1 bedroom 1bath of 610 sq. ft. (57 sq. meters) to 4 bedroom 4- bath of 2050 sq. ft./190 sq. meters.
  • Central air & heat, wall-to-wall carpeting, American-made appliances -. refrigerator, stove, dishwasher, washer and dryer, telephone, cable TV., and balconies.
  • Lobby with granite floors and marble walls, video telephone-entry system and intercom, luxurious stainless steel elevator cab, marble floors & walls on each floor landing, ceramic tile on all exterior walls and floors.
